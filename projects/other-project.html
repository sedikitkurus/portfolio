OTHER PROJECT

I have also worked on several projects related to graphic designing, such as creating logos, booklets, icons and much more. The details of the projects that I had been involved were as follows.

IsoTakon

IsoTakon is an educational application which is aimed at making an easier and more convenient learning process. There are several features in the application such as group learning, notes and summaries of several lessons and learning tutorials. In this project, I re-designed the application.

Pegadaian GIS

Geographic Information System (GIS) of Pegadaian is an internal application of Pegadaian that functions as a surveillance application (WASKAT) or auditing application. In this project, I re-designed the interface of GIS Pegadaian application.

Booklet Baktiku Negeriku - Telkomsel

Baktiku Negeriku is one of the CSR programs from Telkomsel Company. The main purpose of this program is to provide education to rural communities through the use of a digital application platform called Baktiku Negeriku. In this project I designed the activity report of Baktiku Negeriku in form of a booklet.
